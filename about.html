About our company

Proudly Made In Botswana

With 20+ years of printing expertise, we deliver sustainable quality products.

Ourreach Litho (Pty) Ltd. is a 100% manufacturing industry in Botswana specializing in custom manufacturing of paper products. We started as an advertising agency in 2000 and transitioned to manufacturing in 2011-2012. Today we're a fully localized company with 35-53 employees and a 12.2 million BWP investment in our operations.

  • CUSTOM MANUFACTURING:

Specializing in custom products for quantities as low as 20 units.


  • SADC FTA ACCREDITED:

Exporting duty-free to Zimbabwe under SADC Free Trade Agreement.
