About our company

Pioneering Eco-Friendly Stationery Solutions

With 20+ years of printing expertise, we deliver sustainable quality products.

  • 100% Tree-Free Paper Production

  • Fully Customizable Brand Solutions

Based in Gaborone, Botswana, Greencard Diary revolutionizes stationery by using sugarcane bagasse - a sustainable byproduct of sugar production. Our eco-friendly process ensures zero deforestation, with wastewater recycled back to sugarcane farms. From corporate diaries to educational materials, we help brands showcase their environmental commitment.
