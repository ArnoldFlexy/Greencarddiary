Learn How We Deliver Custom Diaries in Just 2 Weeks

In the fast-paced business world of Botswana, timing is everything. While other suppliers take months to deliver custom diaries, Greencard has perfected a streamlined process that gets your branded diaries to you in just 2 weeks. Our rapid turnaround time gives businesses in Gaborone and across Botswana a competitive edge in their marketing and corporate gifting strategies.

Our secret lies in local manufacturing excellence combined with optimized processes. Unlike companies that import diaries from overseas, we produce everything right here in Botswana. This eliminates shipping delays, customs hold-ups, and international logistics challenges that often plague imported products.

Our 2-Week Production Process:

  • Days 1-2: Design consultation and finalization
  • Days 3-5: Material sourcing and preparation
  • Days 6-10: Printing and production
  • Days 11-12: Quality control and finishing
  • Days 13-14: Delivery across Botswana

This accelerated timeline doesn't mean compromised quality. We maintain the highest standards using state-of-the-art printing technology and quality materials. Our team of skilled artisans ensures every diary meets our rigorous quality controls before leaving our facility.

Whether you need diaries for a last-minute corporate event, unexpected client meetings, or forgot to plan your year-end gifts, our 2-week service ensures you never miss an opportunity to make an impression. Many of our clients appreciate this flexibility, especially when working with tight deadlines for product launches or corporate events.

Our fast turnaround is complemented by our low MOQ of just 20 units, making professional branding accessible even for urgent, small-scale needs. For businesses looking to maximize their impact, consider exploring the latest corporate gifting trends in Botswana.
